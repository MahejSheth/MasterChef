INGREDIENTS 
-----
For Pizza dough:
▢ ½ cup warm water
▢ 1 teaspoon sugar
▢ 1 teaspoon dry yeast
▢ 2 cups all-purpose flour
▢ Salt to taste
▢ 3 tablespoon olive oil

For toppings:
▢ 3 tablespoon pizza sauce
▢ 3 tablespoon onion 
▢ ¼ cup capsicum 
▢ 9 pickled jalapeños 
▢ ¼ cup black olives 
▢ ½ cup mozzarella cheese 
▢ ½ teaspoon mixed herbs.
